# Product

## Register

product

## Users

JBL and Jawabu operational staff use the platform inside Telegram and in staff browsers. They create, review, approve, and track workflow records while handling time-sensitive customer and operational data on phones and desktops.

## Product Purpose

The platform provides controlled operational workflows for JBL and Jawabu teams. Each Mini App should make the next valid action obvious, preserve confidence in the workflow state, and keep high-volume staff work fast and readable.

## Brand Personality

Clear, dependable, and practical. The interface should feel calm under pressure, familiar to staff, and focused on completing work rather than decoration.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page styling, oversized decorative surfaces, inconsistent controls between workflows, blocking modal-heavy feedback, and visual density that hides important workflow state.

## Design Principles

1. Make the next operational action clear.
2. Use one coherent mobile-first component vocabulary across Mini Apps.
3. Preserve information density without sacrificing scanning and touch usability.
4. Make loading, success, error, empty, and permission states unmistakable.
5. Let workflow data and staff decisions carry the visual hierarchy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG 2.1 AA contrast expectations, preserve keyboard-accessible controls, and respect reduced-motion preferences. Do not rely on color alone to communicate workflow state.
